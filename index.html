911

Porsche Performance

The new 2025 Porsche 911 delivers a remarkable blend of power and efficiency, making it a standout in its class. Equipped with an 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with Manual Mode, the Porsche 911 offers drivers the versatility to switch between precision automation and engaging manual control. This setup is far superior to the 6-speed manual transmission of the 2025 BMW M4. Plus, the Flat 6 Cylinder Engine, a signature feature of the 911, provides unparalleled performance that isn’t available in either rival.

When it comes to speed, the Porsche 911 is in a league of its own. The 911 Turbo S trim achieves a top track speed of 205 mph, far surpassing the Corvette Stingray (194 mph) and the BMW M4 (191 mph). This incredible performance is powered by a standard turbocharged engine, which enhances acceleration and top-end power. With its precise engineering and exceptional capabilities, the new 2025 Porsche 911 embodies a driving experience that is both exhilarating and efficient.

Porsche Technology

The new 2025 Porsche 911 is equipped with cutting-edge technology that enhances both convenience and entertainment. The class-exclusive Porsche Connect system provides seamless control over navigation, entertainment, and vehicle settings, setting it apart from its competitors. For audiophiles, the 911 offers the choice between a 12-speaker BOSE® Surround Sound System or the top-tier Burmester® High-End Surround Sound System with 23 speakers, both of which surpass the audio systems in the 2025 Chevrolet Corvette Stingray and the 2025 BMW M4.

Safety is another area where the 2025 Porsche 911 excels. With advanced features like Night Vision and Cross-Traffic Alert—unavailable on both the Chevrolet Corvette Stingray and BMW M4—the 911 prioritizes driver and passenger security. The Carrera trim adds even more innovative safety tools, including Forward-Collision Warning, ParkAssist, Porsche Wet Mode, and Adaptive Cruise Control. Combined with the Porsche-signature performance and luxury, the 911 proves to be the ultimate choice for drivers seeking cutting-edge technology and peace of mind.
